Story 4


The Greedy Dog

Once upon a time, there was a dog named Bruno. He was very greedy and always wanted more than he needed. One day, while roaming around the village, he found a juicy bone. He picked it up and ran to a quiet place to enjoy it. As he was crossing a small bridge over a stream, he looked down and saw his reflection in the water. Bruno thought it was another dog with a bigger bone. Greedy as he was, he wanted that bone too. He barked at the reflection, and as he opened his mouth, the bone fell into the water and was carried away by the stream. Bruno was left with nothing and learned a valuable lesson about greed. From that day on, he decided to be satisfied with what he had and not be greedy. Ondu samaya, ondu nayi hesaru Bruno iddanu. Avanu tumba lobha mattu yavattu nidanakannu beku yenta bayasidanu. Ondu dina, halliyannu chikkutta idaga, avanu ondu juicy bone sigitu. Avanu adannu tagedu ondu quiet placege oodutiddanu enjoy madalu. Avanu ondu chikka bridge halla over aagi cross madata idaga, adannu kelage nottu adara reflection water alliyallu. Bruno thinking adu innooru nayi dodda bone ide yenta. Lobha aagi adannu bayasidanu. Avanu reflection mele baayidanu, adannu tanna baye tegi, bone neerina alliyallu bidiyitu halla carried away agalu. Bruno empty handed iruttiddanu mattu lobhada lesson kalitane. A dinadinda, avanu happy iruvudu hesi yaavudu ide, adara mele lobbhe madade.
